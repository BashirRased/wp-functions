WordPress Post Formats:
=======================
01. Standard    – The default post format
02. Aside       – A note like post, usually styled without title.
03. Gallery     – A gallery of images.
04. Link        – A link to another site.
05. Image       – An image or photograph
06. Quote       – A quotation.
07. Status      – Twitter like short status update
08. Video       – A post containing video
09. Audio       – An audio file.
10. Chat        – A chat transcript
